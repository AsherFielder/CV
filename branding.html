Branding

My branding expience includes building the FIELD SNOW brand, and working as a consultant for Chopt Studio.

FIELD was built for a gap in the market for high end minimal ski and snowboard goggles within Australia. The branding for FIELD was based around strong lines, minimal branding and a prodominantly black and white colour palette.

FIELD has been very well recieved by the market, with products being sold within Australia, New Zealand and Japan.

For Chopt Studio I helped refine their aesthetic, built a colour palette for the brand and rebuilt their online experience to reflect these changes.
