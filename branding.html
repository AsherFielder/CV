Branding

In developing the branding for FIELD SNOW I saw a need for clean, minimal and high quality ski and snowboard goggles. I chose to pursue this minimal aesthetic through strong lines in out logos and products, a predominantly black and white colour pallette and a strong social media presence.

I developed In The Field as both a lookbook and a way to improve my website's search engine optimisation. Using this colleciton of images supplied by FIELD's athletes and customers I created a simple but powerful tool for our branding.

Whilst witnessing the climate related disasters during early 2020 in Australia, I decided that FIELD needed to pursue a more environmentally friendly path. After discussions with the factory that FIELD works with, I decided that the risks related to quality control when working with recycled plastics were too high. This is why I developed our tree planting program FIELD GOOD. I commissioned art from Crystal to help spice up the page and help build the programs aesthetic.

I helped develop Chopt Studio's branding through refining their online persona, development of a colour palette and refining Chopt's aesthetic.
