Responding to Feedback

Throughout my digital media studies I have been encouraged by my lecturers to post as much as possible online under the mantra FEFO:

Fail
Early
Fail
Often

The idea of FEFO is to encorage rapid prototyping to make sure a project is on the right track. To rapidly prototype this website, I have shared it on r/Web_Design for feedback.

The majority of the feedback I recieved was to do with changes that I made to the website to protect my privacy, such as removing contact information and limiting the content. Although I found the best feedback was in a reccomended change to the structure of these blog posts. Reducing the font size has made navigation and reading easier.
